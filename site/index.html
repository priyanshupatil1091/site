About Neha’s Retreat

Neha’s Retreat is more than just a villa or farmhouse — it’s an experience wrapped in nature, peace, and personalized hospitality.

Located in the serene hills of Tranquil Town, our retreat offers the perfect blend of luxury and rustic charm. Whether it’s a romantic getaway, a weekend with friends, or a family reunion, we provide the ideal setting for your perfect escape.

With scenic views, modern amenities, and heartwarming service, we ensure that every guest leaves with memories worth cherishing.
